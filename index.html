Plan

Goal Orchestrator

Translates one PM prompt into an execution plan: who to target, what channels to use, how to allocate budget, and where the system should request human approval.

PM touch: setup only

Automates

  • Interprets the market, owner segment, geography, budget, and property filters.
  • Creates the recommended conversion path: data, asset, outreach, nurture, and close sequence.
  • Chooses Observe, Approve, or Autopilot execution checkpoints.

Consumes

  • Goal prompt, brand assets, claims guardrails, calendar destination, spend caps.
  • Historical performance data when available.
  • Compliance rules for channel eligibility and approvals.

Hands off

  • Channel plan, audience priorities, initial budget allocation, and execution graph for downstream agents.
  • Default approval thresholds and exception routing.
  • A 30-day operating plan the PM can inspect without manually configuring the funnel.
Why it matters: this is what makes WanderOS feel like software 3.0 instead of a dashboard full of manual widgets.

02
Find

Property Data & Owner Enrichment Agent

Turns raw market supply into owner-resolved, channel-ready prospect lists. This productizes the exact workflow Wander has already proven manually.

PM touch: near-zero

Automates

  • Ingests AirDNA-style STR supply and local MLS inventory via feeds, connectors, or approved scrape workflows.
  • Normalizes addresses, dedupes listings, resolves parcel or assessor records, and distinguishes likely direct owners from managers or entities.
  • Enriches phone, email, and mailing address data, applies suppression rules, and confidence-scores every record.

Consumes

  • Market filters, bedroom / quality filters, STR listing supply, local MLS data, assessor / tax sources, and exclusion lists.
  • YellowPages-style enrichment services or equivalent contact vendors.
  • Existing owners, opt-outs, DNC / suppression records, and conversion feedback from prior campaigns.

Hands off

  • Ranked owner lists for lifecycle nurture, sales-call queues, direct-mail drops, and paid retargeting audiences.
  • Source provenance, match confidence, owner type labels, and segment recommendations.
  • Always-on replenishment of the owner acquisition pipeline as new supply appears.
Why it matters: revenue calculators can lift conversion, but they do not create the list. The upstream wedge is owner resolution plus contact enrichment.

Breakout agent

The owner-resolution engine is the operational wedge.

This is the piece that most competitors still leave fragmented across vendors, spreadsheets, and manual labor. WanderOS should productize it end to end.

Manual Wander playbook, now productized: buy or source a large market supply set, identify the direct owner through county / parcel / tax data, enrich that owner record, then route the opportunity into cold email, direct mail, call queues, retargeting, and lifecycle nurture. In WanderOS, all of that becomes one agentic workflow.

Step 01

Ingest supply

Pull STR inventory, MLS listings, and market-level home supply using the target filters that came from the PM prompt.

Step 02

Normalize and dedupe

Clean addresses, match listing variants, group by parcel or property identity, and suppress duplicate opportunities.

Step 03

Resolve ownership

Use assessor, parcel, or tax data to identify the likely owner and separate direct owners from PM-controlled entities.

Step 04

Enrich contact data

Attach likely phones, emails, and mailing addresses using enrichment vendors and confidence-scored matching.

Step 05

Score and route

Rank fit, confidence, and intent, then send the right records to lifecycle, direct mail, SDR, and paid retargeting.

STR listing supply

AirDNA-style inventory or comparable sources to identify homes already operating in the short-term rental market.

MLS inventory

Feeds, connectors, or approved scrape workflows to discover newly listed properties or homes with likely STR potential.

Assessor / tax data

Parcel-level ownership resolution to identify the likely direct owner behind the listing.

Contact enrichment

YellowPages-style enrichment or equivalent vendors to attach outreach-ready emails, phones, and mailing addresses.

Suppressions

Existing clients, opt-outs, DNC records, bad contacts, and prior conversions to keep the system safe and efficient.

Trust model

Hands-off does not mean out of control.

A PM needs to feel like the system is doing the work without risking spend, brand claims, data quality, or close-stage approvals.

Observe modeGreat for the first run. Agents build the market list, plan the funnel, and draft the assets, but nothing launches without a green light.
Approve modeDefault for most PMs. Agents can enrich data, prepare campaigns, and manage lifecycle logic, while launch thresholds and contract sends still require approval.
Autopilot modeFor high-trust accounts. Agents operate within budget caps, claim libraries, source rules, and escalation policies, only surfacing exceptions.

Budget control

Daily and monthly spend caps, change thresholds, and automatic pacing rules for paid channels.

Brand-safe claims

Only approved proof points, testimonials, and performance language can be used in pages, ads, emails, and mailers.

Data provenance

Every owner record should retain source lineage, match confidence, and suppression status before it enters outreach.

Compliance layer

Eligibility checks, suppression management, consent logic, channel restrictions, and human approval for contract-stage materials.

What success looks like

Measure signed homes, speed, data quality, and autonomous execution — not just leads.

The system should be evaluated on whether it compounds a PM’s business, not whether it generated a pile of unqualified activity.

Inventory growth

Net new signed homes, new AUM, incremental booking value added, and cost per signed home.

Speed

Prompt-to-plan time, prompt-to-first-live-campaign time, lead response time, and days to first booked meeting.

Data quality

Listing coverage, owner-resolution rate, enrichment coverage, suppression accuracy, and bounce / bad-number rate.

Autonomy

Share of tasks completed without PM intervention, campaigns self-optimized, and exception rate by workstream.

Conversion

Lead-to-meeting, meeting-to-proposal, proposal-to-signed-home, and channel-level CAC with payback.

System health

Page conversion, email / SMS engagement, ad efficiency, mail response rate, and attribution confidence.
